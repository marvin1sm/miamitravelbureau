About Miami Travel Bureau

A boutique, bilingual tour service focused on art, culture and real conversations instead of generic sightseeing.

The Idea Behind Miami Travel Bureau

Miami Travel Bureau was born from years of guiding travelers in Havana, Santiago and Miami, and seeing how much people appreciate honest local context and flexible itineraries.

Instead of big buses and megaphones, you get a small‑scale, private experience hosted by someone who actually lives here and works between languages and cultures every day.

What You Can Expect

  • Private or small‑group experiences only
  • Bilingual guiding (Spanish, English, Japanese or Portuguese)
  • Flexible itineraries built around your interests
  • Respectful storytelling around Miami’s communities
  • Help with logistics, reservations and local tips

Your Guide

Lee Marvin Segundo is a tour guide, travel planner and translator who has worked with guests from all over the world. He combines a background in sports and languages with a love for street art, music and neighborhood stories.

He has designed experiences for independent travelers, families, travel agencies and brands looking for a deeper, more human view of Miami.
