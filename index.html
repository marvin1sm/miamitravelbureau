About Us

Welcome to Miami Travel Bureau.

A locally rooted boutique travel experience company that blends Miami’s vibrant culture, street art, and coastal lifestyle into unforgettable half-day adventures.

Founded by Lee, a multilingual tour designer and cultural concierge originally from Cuba, who has spent over a decade helping visitors see Miami the way locals do.

Our goal is simple: to turn travel into a meaningful connection. From Wynwood murals to Little Havana cafecitos, every experience is handcrafted to show you the soul of the city, not just its skyline.
