Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect the details you share when you contact us or request a booking, such as your name, email, phone number, preferred dates, group size, tour edition, and any notes you choose to provide. Our website may also capture basic usage data (for example, cookies or analytics) to improve performance and usability.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to reply to inquiries, manage bookings, personalize the experience, and communicate updates or changes. We also analyze aggregate, non-identifying usage data to refine our services and website.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited details with trusted third parties only as needed to deliver your tour—for instance, transportation, ticketing, or venue partners—under appropriate confidentiality and security expectations.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by emailing info@miamitravelbureau.com. If you disable cookies in your browser, portions of the site may not function as intended.

Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page and will indicate the effective date of changes.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email info@miamitravelbureau.com.
